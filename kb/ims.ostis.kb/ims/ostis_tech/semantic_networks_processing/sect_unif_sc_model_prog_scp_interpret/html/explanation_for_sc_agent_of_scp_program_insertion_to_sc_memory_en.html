sc-agent of scp-program insertion to sc-memory is activated the limited number of times in case of appearance in sc-memory earlier not being present scp-program, executes with it certain actions, whereupon scp-program becomes available for interpretation, or marked as incorrect scp-program.Thereafter sc-agent of scp-program insertion to sc-memory doesn't work with the specified scp-program any more, i.e. process of its operation from the point of view of process of interpretation can be considered as one-fold operation, executed beforehand for everyone scp-program and not having essential impact on system performance as a whole.
