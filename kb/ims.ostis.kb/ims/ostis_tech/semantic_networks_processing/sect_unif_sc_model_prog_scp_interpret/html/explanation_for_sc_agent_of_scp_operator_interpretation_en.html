Task of the sc-agent of scp-operator interpretation is interpretation of the scp-program'soperators, that is execution in sc-memory actions,which described by the specific scp-operator. This sc-agent is activated in case of appearance in sc-memory of the scp- operator, inhering to the class of the active sc-operators. After execution of the actions are described with scp-operator, scp-operator is added in a set of executed scp-operator.
